If you need to know how many deaths you have before you get sent to the depths, look at your health bar. If it looks corroded that means you are 1 death away.

If you want to make a build plan, you can use the deepwoken builder website.

When you are a skilled player, you can progress in the depths. You get significantly more EXP, allowing a full build to be progged in less then an hour.

Central Authority origin allows certain weapons to be used without their requirment.

Equipment is very important, without good equipment you will not be able to compete.

Never underestimate your opponent, PvE and PvP. The tables may turn very quickly.

Shrines are very important for minmaxing your build, they use the currency "knowledge".

To learn the basics of PvE, spamming trial of one will help.

Certain boons and races give investment points on power up, these allow you to put points into stats without using their exp.
