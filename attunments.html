Attunmentless

Allows the user to obtain the Oath: "Silentheart". Also grants an additional 10 investment points.

Flamecharm

High damage, high ether cost. When used in a good build, Flamecharm is one of the strongest attunments.

Frostdraw

Low damage, but applies freeze. Freeze slows enemies, which can seriously disorient them.

Galebreath

Applies the "winded" effect. This effect makes the enemy not able to use any mantras. Gale also has high damage potential, very strong when used correctly.

Thunder

Stuns enemies on hit. Most players know how to counter stun, primarly used for PvE.

Shadowcast

Drains enemies ether. If the enemy does not reserve ether well then they have zero chance of winning.

Ironsing

Incredibly high damage along with bleed. Hard to use, but does insane damage in both PvE and PvP.

Bloodrend

Has lifesteal, does high damage, and has low ether cost. Extremly strong, but at the cost of blood drain on the use of moves.
