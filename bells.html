Resonances, commonly called Bells, are individual unique abilities the player character can obtain through a near-death experience in the depths.

When you have gained enough resonance exp and you die in the depths, you will be revived coming back with full health and now a bell.

Your bell represents the players connection with the song, and they can be quite strong when used correctly.
