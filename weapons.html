Heavy

High damage, high posture damage, slow swing speed.

---

Works well with high damaging attunments that don't do much posture damage.

Can daze enemies, gains a lot of flourish proc talents.

---

Heavy weapons consist of:

- Greataxes

- Greatswords

- Greathammers

- Greatcannons

Medium

Medium damage, medium posture damage, medium swing speed.

---

Works well with bassically all builds, good middle ground.

Not used much so it can throw enemies off as they will not be as experienced.

---

Medium weapons consist of:

- Swords

- Spears

- Clubs

- Rifles

- Twinblades

- Bows

Light

Low damage, bassically zero posture damage, insanly fast swing speed.

---

Works well with attunments that can easily block break.

Swing speed is so fast that it's quite hard to parry, can land sneaky m1s to proc talents to increase damage.

---

Light weapons consist of:

- Dagger

- Fist

- Pistol

- Rapier
