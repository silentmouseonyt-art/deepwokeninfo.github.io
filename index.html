Deepwoken is a brutal, open-world roguelike RPG.

It's well known for it's in depth mechanics, lore, and of course PvP.

Deepwoken has a complex parry-based combat system, being based off your build.

This may seem "bad" but it gives the player complete creativity and control over how they want to play the game.

For example, if you're very confident in your parrying skills you could do a glass cannon build.

On the other hand, if you feel your parrying skills are terrible you could do a tank build.

Regardless of what you pick, most of your builds will end up the same. Wiped.

Deepwoken has a brutal permadeath mechanic. If you die twice, you get sent to "The Depths".

If you die there, that build is gone forever.
